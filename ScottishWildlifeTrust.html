Scottish Wildlife Trust

The Scottish Wildlife Trust is a charity that devotes itself to conserving the wildlife and habitats of animals in Scotland.
The goal of this charity is to protect wildlife reserves and save species from going extinct. The hopes to save these animals is
taking a new form in the development of this game as it is a new type of media the charity is branching out to in its
never ending efforts to spread awareness.
