Game Information

This game was made with the purpose of spreading awareness for the Scottish Wildlife Trust. The goal is to provide a simple and fun game
suitable for a younger audience to play so that the message of what the trust aims to achieve; through this form of advertisement, the orginisation
will now reach a wider demographic which can strengthen the plans they wish to carry out.
